Alpha-Flow

Positioning Statement

Alpha-Flow is an AI-powered financial education platform that teaches people how to understand markets, evaluate opportunities, and make better investment decisions through personalized coaching, interactive simulations, and explainable AI.

---

Mission

Help millions of people become confident, disciplined investors by teaching them how to think rather than what to buy.

---

Vision

Create the world's most trusted AI financial mentor—one that develops independent decision-makers instead of followers.

---

Who It's For

Beginners

People who want to learn investing from the ground up without being overwhelmed by jargon.

Self-Directed Investors

Individuals looking to improve their research, portfolio management, and long-term decision-making.

Active Traders

People who want to strengthen technical analysis, risk management, and trading discipline.

Students

High school, college, and university learners studying finance, economics, or investing.

Professionals

Engineers, entrepreneurs, business owners, and career changers who want to build financial literacy.

---

What Alpha-Flow Does

- Explains why markets move.
- Teaches investing and trading concepts in plain language.
- Builds personalized learning paths.
- Reviews simulated trades.
- Creates quizzes and interactive lessons.
- Explains charts step by step.
- Tracks learning progress.
- Encourages disciplined risk management.
- Adapts to each learner's experience level.

---

What Alpha-Flow Does NOT Do

- Promise profits.
- Guarantee successful trades.
- Encourage gambling behavior.
- Tell users to blindly buy or sell assets.
- Replace licensed financial professionals.

---

Core Learning Loop

1. Learn

Short, focused lessons tailored to the learner's experience level.

2. Observe

Analyze real market data while explaining the reasoning behind price movements.

3. Practice

Make simulated investment or trading decisions in a risk-free environment.

4. Reflect

Receive detailed feedback on reasoning, risk assessment, and trade planning.

5. Improve

Generate personalized lessons based on strengths and weaknesses.

---

AI Mentor Modules

Market Teacher

Explains current market events in everyday language.

Examples:

- Why did the market fall today?
- Why are interest rates important?
- What happens when inflation rises?

---

Chart Tutor

Guides users through reading charts and technical indicators.

Topics include:

- Candlesticks
- Trend lines
- Support and resistance
- Volume
- Moving averages
- RSI
- MACD
- Bollinger Bands

---

Strategy Coach

Introduces different investing and trading approaches.

Examples:

- Trend following
- Momentum investing
- Mean reversion
- Value investing
- Growth investing
- Swing trading
- Position trading

Rather than recommending one strategy, Alpha-Flow helps learners understand when each may be appropriate and what trade-offs it involves.

---

Risk Mentor

Builds habits that protect capital.

Lessons include:

- Position sizing
- Stop losses
- Risk/reward analysis
- Portfolio diversification
- Drawdowns
- Emotional discipline

---

Practice Lab

Learners can:

- Replay historical markets.
- Make simulated trades.
- Build portfolios.
- Test strategies.
- Learn from mistakes without risking real money.

---

Lesson Generator

Creates personalized study plans based on:

- Knowledge level
- Quiz results
- Simulation performance
- Areas needing improvement
- Learning goals

---

Explain Why Framework

Every analysis follows the same structure:

Observation

What is happening?

Evidence

What facts support this conclusion?

Alternative Views

What other interpretations exist?

Risks

What could invalidate this analysis?

Confidence

How certain is the explanation, and why?

Next Learning Step

What concept should the learner explore next?

# Run and deploy your AI Studio app

This contains everything you need to run your app locally.

View your app in AI Studio: https://ai.studio/apps/2919f9e1-3f77-49a7-85bd-d186f465ce5c

## Run Locally

**Prerequisites:**  Node.js


1. Install dependencies:
   `npm install`
2. Set the `GEMINI_API_KEY` in [.env.local](.env.local) to your Gemini API key
3. Run the app:
   `npm run dev`
